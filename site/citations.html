Note on Source Application

The sources listed above provided theoretical foundations, established geometric ratios, and design principles. This project represents an independent synthesis and application of these principles to passive ventilation design. All performance claims are theoretical and based on published engineering relationships rather than experimental validation specific to this configuration.

Sources were accessed through academic databases, engineering handbooks, and publicly available technical literature between 2024-2025. Where specific design ratios or coefficients are cited in the technical sections, they reference established values documented in these sources.
