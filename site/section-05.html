Micro-Vortex Generators

Micro-Scale Vortex Generators (MVG)

MVG Sizing

10%–100% of local boundary layer thickness (Choosing 80%)

  • Taller → parasitic drag
  • Short → cannot draw momentum air towards wall

Airfoil profiling gives ~27.68% reduction in drag and stronger vortices downstream.

Spacing Ratio

λ / h = 3 to 5 (Choosing 5)

Prevents flow separation, ensures air fills full 153 cm exit instead of leaving as narrow turbulent jet.

Placement Ratio in Conical Diffuser

x / L = 0.05 to 0.10

  • x = distance from start of diffuser
  • L = total diffuser length

x = 153 × 0.05 = 7.65 cm

x = 153 × 0.10 = 15.3 cm

→ Place MVG ~15 cm down diffuser

MVG Placement, Spacing, and Offset Logic

Offset Configuration

15° offset from the vane axis

This allows MVG receives undisturbed air at maximum velocity and highest energy.

Circumferential Layout

Circumference of diffuser at 15 cm down:

≈ 94.8 × π ≈ 297.8 cm

To offset, move 12.4 cm away from any vane line, place first MVG pair, and then add the remaining 11 MVG pairs after every 24.8 cm.
